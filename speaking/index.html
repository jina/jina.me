Speaking

I Love Speaking!

I’ve been speaking at events for 10 years across 15 countries, 41 cities, and online. I really enjoy it a lot. Lately, I feel fortunate to have had many great opportunities to speak, learn and travel. If I could, I would accept every invitation to speak!

However, I really love my job, and I want to be good at it! So I need to limit the engagements I take on each year. To help make the process as smooth as possible), I’ve put together this list of what we can expect from each other. If all looks good, get in touch!

What to Expect

Your Event

  • Your event must have a Code of Conduct for me to accept the invitation.
  • Let me know in advance if talks are recorded.
  • Tell me about your audience (creative, technical, skill levels, etc).
  • I will wait until your event gets the opportunity to make the announcement, and then I will promote my speaking engagement at your event on Twitter, Facebook, and my website.
  • Do not schedule me for 1-on-1 consultations with your attendees without my explicit, written consent.

My Presentation

  • Let me know in advance how much time I have.
  • My content will be up-to-date with current standards and best practices, to the best of my knowledge.
  • I will design my slides myself on my own template.
  • Help me get set up with A/V. Usually it’s not a problem, but in the past I have been left to set myself up and was delayed due to technical difficulties against my control.
  • I will present on my own computer, and I will advance my own slides.
  • I won’t do live code demos or design battles.
  • I will not participate in anything involving a “battle of the sexes”. I made this mistake early in my career. Never again.
  • I will publish my slides and tweet out the link.

Compensation & Accommodations

  • Unless you are a non-profit, I require a speaker honorarium.
  • I will give priority to events that cover my travel and accommodations. I would like to arrive at least one day before the event begins, and depart at least one day after the event ends, unless I request otherwise. This is so I can see your full event! Please cover this and all days of the event. If I decide to stay additional days, I will cover that on my own.
  • If Salesforce covers my travel and accommodations, I ask that you please indicate them as a sponsor.

Let’s Talk

If you would like me to speak at your event, please contact me with:

  • short description of your conference
  • event date and location
  • talk type (keynote/session, workshop, panel…)
  • speaker honorarium and travel arrangements
